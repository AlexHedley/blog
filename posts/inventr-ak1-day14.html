Today was an amalgamation of Day 11, Day 12 and Day 13 so we can combine sound, light and the keypad.

PWM ~ is key on the board.

Parts

  • Hero Board
  • Type B USB Cable
  • Breadboard
  • 16 cables (M-M)
  • Keypad
  • Passive Buzzer
  • 3 220 Ω (Ohm) resistor
  • 3 colour LED or RGB LED
